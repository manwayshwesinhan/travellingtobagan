Popular Festivals

Ananda Temple Festival

Held between December and January, in the Burmese month of Pyatho.

Manuha Pagoda Festival

Villagers celebrate this festival in September at Myinkaba Village, Bagan.

Water Festival

Held April, in the Burmese month of Tagoo.

Thadingyut Festival

Held held on the full moon of Thadingyut in October.
